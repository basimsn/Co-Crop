Planting

Starting your own garden is simple and rewarding! First, pick a good spot with lots of sunshine and water. Then, dig up your soil with a fork or tiller to loosen the soil. We recommend adding manuer or other nutrients during this step. When it comes to chosing what to plant, it's important to consider your sourounding climate. Place the seeds in your dug up soil and water it so that the soil is dam. Continue to water your plants daily and look out for bugs and sickness. Finally, when you're ready to harvest, enjoy your produce and sell your extras here!

Upkeep

Taking care of your plants and veggies is super important for making sure they grow big and strong. Start by giving them enough sun and water. Don't forget to water them regularly, but don't drown them! Putting some mulch around them can help keep the dirt nice and moist and keep the weeds away. Also, feed them every now and then with some good compost or plant food to give them the nutrients they need. Keep an eye out for bugs and sickness, 'cause catching it early can save your plants. Try using natural stuff or organic bug spray to keep those pests away. Lastly, trim your plants when they need it to help them grow better. Following these steps will help your plants and veggies grow awesome!
